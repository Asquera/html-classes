Lesson 1: Familiarize yourself with basic HTML

Get your tools ready

To follow this class, you need at least these tools:

  • A text editor (TextMate and Coda are both good options)
  • Your preferred Browser

Later on, we will learn about other tools, like tidy, the W3C Validator and your preferred browsers developer support, so go ahead and have a look at them if you are interested.

Terminology

Source
The HTML document as displayed in your editor.
